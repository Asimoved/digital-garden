learn

/ləːn/

Old English leornian ‘learn’ (in Middle English also ‘teach’), of West Germanic origin; related to German lernen, also to lore^1

Definitions

  • verb
    • become aware of (something) by information or from observation.
    • To fix in the mind or memory; memorize.
    • To become aware or informed of; find out: synonym: discover.
    • To gain knowledge of or skill in through study, instruction, or experience.
