refer

/rɪˈfəː/

late Middle English: from Old French referer or Latin referre ‘carry back’, from re- ‘back’ + ferre ‘bring’.

Definitions:

  • verb
    • Archaic trace or attribute something to (someone or something) as a cause or source.
    • mention or [allude] to.
    • pass a matter to (a higher body) for a decision.
